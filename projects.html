  3. Conway's Game of Life (Python)

    August, 2022 - August, 2022

    Recently, I discovered something called "Game of Life", this game/simulation was invented by John Horton Conway in 1970. The concept is simple, it is a "zero player game" and there are two states, dead and alive. To be alive or dead, a cell check the neighbors, if the neighbors alive is more or less than a certain number, the cell dies. if a dead cell is surrounded by a key number of cells, it becomes alive. it is a simple concept but very fun to code it. I'll probably do it in other programming language in the future.

    October, 2022 - May, 2023
